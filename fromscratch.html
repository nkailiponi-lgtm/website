my favorite mtg commanders

Commander is my favorite Magic: The Gathering format because it allows for creativity, unique strategies, and fun multiplayer interactions. These commanders represent different playstyles that I enjoy and have spent time building and refining.

Pako and Haldan

Pako, Arcane Retriever and Haldan, Avid Arcanist work together to exile cards from opponents and let me play them. Pako grows stronger every time he attacks, and Haldan allows me to cast the exiled cards for value.

Volo, Guide to Monsters

Volo rewards building a deck with unique creature types. When creatures enter the battlefield, Volo copies them, creating powerful board states and lots of value.

Aesi, Tyrant of Gyre Strait

Aesi focuses on landfall and card advantage. By playing extra lands each turn, the deck ramps quickly and overwhelms opponents through value.
